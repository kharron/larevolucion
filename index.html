Living on the edge, Living the dream

La Revolucion seats on The Edge of 80 acres of pristine Tropical Dry forest of the award winning Escamequita Nature Reserve.

The Reserve was awarded the Gold Standard by both the Rain forest Alliance and the CCBA (Climate Community & Biodiversity Alliance) We are 10 minutes form San Juan Del Sur on a Paved road and another 10 minutes from 3 different world class surf breaks.
